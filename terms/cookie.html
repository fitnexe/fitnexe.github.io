What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file saved on your computer or mobile device by a website when you visit the website. The cookie enables the website to remember your actions and preferences such as login, language, font size and other display preferences to keep you from having to reenter them on every visit to the website or when browsing from page to page.

How do we use cookies?
